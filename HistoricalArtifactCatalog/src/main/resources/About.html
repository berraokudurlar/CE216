About HistoFact

HistoFact is a historical artifact management application developed as the final semester project by three IUE students — Ege Çakıcı, Zeynep Erman, and Berra Okudurlar — for the CE 216 class.

Designed to simplify the documentation, exploration, and curation of artifacts, HistoFact enables users to create, organize, and visually explore historical records with ease and clarity.
